On October 30, 2023, the White House issued Executive Order (E.O.) 14110 on Safe, Secure, and Trustworthy Development and Use of Artificial Intelligence.

The E.O. marks a governmentwide effort to guide responsible artificial intelligence (AI) development and deployment.

This site hosts guidance and data tools designed to help answer common questions around bringing AI to a federal workplace, with one important caveat:

GenAI is not all of AI

The guidance is focused on generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) applications and related infrastructure. What's the difference?

Who's it for?

The guidance is directed to members of the federal acquisition workforce.

Is it final?

No. This is Version 1.0, it will evolve over time as agencies share their GenAI needs and insights.

This initiative runs on your feedback, learn how to contribute.
