On October 30, 2023, the White House issued Executive Order (E.O.) 14110 on Safe, Secure, and Trustworthy Development and Use of Artificial Intelligence.

The E.O. marks a governmentwide effort to guide responsible artificial intelligence (AI) development and deployment. This website hosts guidance and data tools designed to answer common questions around bringing AI to a federal workplace.
